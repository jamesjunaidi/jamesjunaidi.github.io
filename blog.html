Finished with my first year at UCD!

Updated June 6, 2020

Yesterday, I finished my first year at UC Davis as a CS major! I wanted to write to reflect a little on how my first year went. So firstly, a major change from the start of the year to now is that I switched from the CSE to the CS major, and I now also have plans to get a minor in econ. This is something that I only decided after my winter quarter. The CS classes I took were ECS 36A, 36B, 36C, 20, and 50. I felt like i grew a lot as a CS major after this one year. I additionally worked on some cool projects on different platforms, like Komma for android, iOS, and web application. I also worked on a webpage in the #include club. I also participated in LAHacks, where me and some friends built a shopping inventory alerter in Python using the Twilio API. I grew a lot more than I thought I could as a software developor over this year. Now, I'm super excited to start my internship at T-Mobile in just about a week and a half!
